Welcome to GitHub Pages.

Welcome to my page! I am a first year Master's student at the Georgia Instititue of Technology. I am currently enrolled in the Bioinformatics Professional Science Master's Program. I graduated from ECU in 2013 and double majored in Biology/Chemistry with 6 months of lab experience in a Genomics lab at East Carolina University. My academic background includes intensive research-oriented study and hands on experience. I have a deep interest in computer programming and have completed MOOC courses in programming with python and continue to take further programming courses online out of this interest. Topics of interest to me include Epidemiology, Microbial Pathogenesis, Data Visualization, k-mer techniques for sequencing and data processing. Currently I am a graduate research assistant with the Vannberg lab, working on designing and testing a primer design tool utilizing k-mers and De Bruijn principles for use with real time PCR. Other projects I have been invovled with include d3 data visualization to demonstrate genetic similarity based on kmer counts. of various human pathogens. GitHub
